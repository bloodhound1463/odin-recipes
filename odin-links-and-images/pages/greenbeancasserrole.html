Green Bean Casserole

>

Ingredients

  • 1 can (10 1/2 ounces) Condensed Cream of Mushroom Soup or 98% Fat Free Cream of Mushroom Soup or Condensed Unsalted Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 4 cups cooked cut green beans
  • 1 1/3 cups French'sÂ® French Fried Onions (amount divided in recipe steps below)

Steps

  1. Heat oven 350 degrees. Stir the soup, soy sauce, milk, beans and 2/3 cup of onions in a 1 1/2-quart casserole.
  2. Bake for 25 minutes or til hot. Stir the bean mixture. Now sprinkle the remaining 2/3 cup onions.
  3. Bake for an additional 5 minutes or until the onions are golden brown.
